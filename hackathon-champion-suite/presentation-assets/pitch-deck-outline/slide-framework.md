# 🎤 The Winning 3-Minute Pitch - Slide-by-Slide

> Total: 7 slides, ~25s each. Lead with the demo. Practice 3x out loud.

## Slide 1 - Title + Hook (0:00-0:20)
- Project name, one-line tagline, team.
- **Open with a hook**, not "hi we're team X": a stat, a question, or a bold claim.
- *"Every year, $X is wasted on ___. We fixed it in 24 hours."*

## Slide 2 - The Problem (0:20-0:45)
- One relatable user + their pain. Make the judges *feel* it.
- One number that proves the problem is big.

## Slide 3 - The Solution (0:45-1:05)
- Your one-sentence pitch. What it does + the magic moment.
- Keep it concrete: "It does X so users get Y."

## Slide 4 - LIVE DEMO (1:05-2:05) ⭐ the heart
- **Switch to the live app.** Walk the single golden path.
- Narrate the *value*, not the clicks: "Notice it updated in real time..."
- Backup video ready if wifi dies.

## Slide 5 - How It Works / Tech (2:05-2:30)
- Architecture diagram. Name the impressive tech (real-time WS, AI, etc.).
- Call out the *one hard thing* you solved. Judges reward visible depth.

## Slide 6 - Impact & What's Next (2:30-2:50)
- Who it helps + the big vision in one breath.
- "Today it does X; in 3 months it becomes Y."

## Slide 7 - The Ask / Close (2:50-3:00)
- Team + live URL + repo, big on screen.
- End on the tagline. Confident. Smile. Stop talking.

## Delivery rules
- One presenter drives slides, one drives the demo.
- No reading slides. Slides = visuals, you = the story.
- Rehearsed > perfect. Calm energy wins.
